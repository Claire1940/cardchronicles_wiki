While carry cards provide the raw damage needed to defeat bosses, support and defensive cards ensure your squad survives long encounters. Interestingly, support cards in Card Chronicles can also have borders (such as Gold, Magmatic, or Abyssal), which scale their passive benefits dramatically.

| Card Name | Core Defense Role | Key Passive Mechanic | Team Fit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Woven Star** | Stall Tank | Stays at 1 HP for extended duration | Defense Lineups |
| **Fairy Strategist** | Buff Support | Critical attack and speed amplifier | Universal Staple |
| **Binding Light** | Control/Utility | Skill disable and crowd control | Universal Staple |
| **Mother of Creation** | Disabler | Negates enemy card abilities | Attack Lineups |
| **Integrity Knight** | Counter-Disabler | Negates enemy skill negation | Meta Counters |

For example, **Death Surgeon** is a fantastic defensive support card that grants a dodge chance when your team's HP drops below 75%. At base rarity, this dodge chance is only 1%, but it scales up to 7.5% at Abyssal rarity, making it an incredibly powerful option for late-game wave survival.

<Callout type="warning" title="Defense Warning">
Do not neglect your support cards. Even if a support card has lower base stats, its passive mechanics like dodge chance or skill disables can completely turn the tide of a high-difficulty wave battle.
</Callout>
