The core loop of the **card chronicles roblox game** revolves around the "Roll" button. Every roll gives you a chance to obtain a new anime-style card. While the base luck might seem low, the game provides multiple layers of "Luck" stacking to help you find Mythic and Legendary units.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Lineup Logic and Team Building">

Building a team in **Card Chronicles** is about balance. You cannot simply stack five high-damage cards and expect to win. You need to consider how your cards interact. A balanced lineup usually consists of one primary carry, one secondary damage dealer, and three support or utility cards that provide buffs or crowd control.

| Role | Responsibility | Recommended Traits |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Main Carry** | Clear the bulk of the wave | Godly, Celestial, Power |
| **Boss Killer** | High single-target DPS | Crit, Sharp, Focused |
| **Support** | Buff allies or debuff foes | Swift, Haste, Aura |
| **Tank/Staller** | Prevent enemies from passing | Heavy, Shielded, Vitality |

The placement of your cards in the lineup can also affect their performance. Some abilities have a specific range or affect adjacent cards. Always read the "Lineup Bonus" section of your card's stats to see if they gain extra power when placed next to specific types or rarities.
