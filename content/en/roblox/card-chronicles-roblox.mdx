### Tier Breakdown & Investment Strategy

- **S-Tier (Main Carries)**: These cards feature massive area-of-effect (AoE) damage and powerful passive abilities. They scale exceptionally well with upgrades and are the only cards worthy of your precious Trait Rerolls.
- **A-Tier (Reliable Progression)**: Strong, dependable cards that can carry you through dozens of wave walls. They perform well even without perfect traits, making them highly cost-effective.
- **B-Tier (Specialists)**: These units excel in niche situations, such as single-target boss fights, but struggle against rapid, multi-enemy waves. Upgrade them only if your main lineup lacks single-target damage.
- **C-Tier (Placeholders)**: Low-rarity cards used to fill slots during your first few minutes of gameplay. Do not waste gold or rerolls on these units; replace them as soon as you pull an A-tier or S-tier card.

<Callout type="success" title="Upgrade Priority">
Always maximize the level of your primary S-tier carry before distributing resources to secondary units. A single over-leveled carry card will clear waves much faster than a balanced team of low-level cards.
</Callout>
