<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait Rerolls and Ability Upgrades">

Traits are randomized modifiers that can drastically change a card's performance. Since Trait Rerolls are a limited resource (often obtained via codes or rare drops), you must be surgical in how you use them. Upgrading your cards is a prerequisite for high-level play, as it unlocks the hidden potential of your anime-style fighters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Keeper Cards">
    Only use rerolls on cards that you intend to keep in your lineup for at least 100+ waves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Ability Synergies">
    Some traits boost specific damage types. Ensure your trait matches the card's primary ability for maximum impact.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Rerolls in Sessions">
    Redeem your code-based rerolls all at once and focus on one card until it hits a high-tier trait like "Godly" or "Eternal."
  </Step>
</Steps>

| System | Purpose | Best Practice |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rolling** | Finding new cards | Use Luck Potions in 15-min bursts |
| **Upgrading** | Increasing base stats | Focus on your main carry first |
| **Traits** | Adding percentage buffs | Save for S-tier cards only |
| **Abilities** | Combat mechanics | Unlock via level milestones |

<Callout type="info" title="Trait Priority Logic">
If you have a limited number of rerolls, prioritize **Damage %** and **Critical Chance** traits. In the current 2026 meta, offensive power is significantly more valuable than defensive traits for clearing endless waves.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Potion Management and FAQ">

Potions are the lifeblood of efficient progression. Using a Luck Potion while you are not actively rolling is a waste of a rare resource. Similarly, Battle Speed potions should only be used when your lineup is strong enough to clear waves quickly, as they don't provide a direct power boost—only a time-saving one.

| Potion Type | Category | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Luck Potion** | Rolling | Use when hunting for S-tier cards |
| **Roll Speed** | Rolling | Pair with Luck Potions for max volume |
| **Battle Speed** | Combat | Use during easy farm waves to save time |
| **Boss Potion** | Combat | Use when attempting to break a boss wall |
| **Super Luck** | Rolling | Save for limited-time events or new releases |

<Callout type="info" title="Boost Stacking">
You can stack different types of potions (e.g., Luck + Roll Speed), but stacking two of the same potion usually just extends the duration rather than doubling the effect. Always check the active buff icons before consuming more.
</Callout>
