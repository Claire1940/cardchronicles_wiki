The core progression loop is simple but requires strategy. When you start, your primary task is generating rolls. Every roll increases your total roll count, which is the primary gatekeeper for unlocking automation features. For instance, auto-roll becomes available once you hit 25 rolls, while quick roll unlocks at 100 rolls. This automation allows you to cycle through cards rapidly, looking for high-rarity carries.

Beyond rolling, you must actively participate in battles. Pushing through endless waves of foes allows you to test your lineup's strength and earn progression rewards. Additionally, keep an eye on the map for boss spawns. Map bosses spawn every hour and offer a reliable way to farm potions, weather rerolls, and exclusive character cards.

| System | Purpose | Primary Resource |
|---|---|---|
| **Rolling** | Expand card collection and find rare anime carries | Roll charges |
| **Upgrading** | Increase card level and unlock combat abilities | Duplicates / Gold |
| **Constellations** | Gain permanent stat boosts like luck and damage | Stars (125k rolls) |
| **Map Bosses** | Farm progression potions and rare drop cards | Boss Spawns (Hourly) |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Starter Progression">

Navigating the early game of Card Chronicles can be overwhelming due to the sheer volume of cards and resources thrown your way. To build a powerful roster quickly, you must follow a structured path. This progression route ensures you do not waste valuable potions or rerolls on temporary placeholders.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Automation Milestones">
    Begin by rolling continuously to hit the 25 and 100 roll milestones. This unlocks auto-roll and quick roll, allowing you to cycle through cards efficiently without manual effort.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assemble a Baseline Lineup">
    Equip your first five cards. Focus on high-rarity pulls or upgraded commons that offer decent base damage. Do not worry about perfect synergy yet; raw damage is key for early wave clearing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Merge Duplicates for Power">
    Merge duplicate cards immediately to strengthen your active roster. Merging increases base stats and helps you push past early wave walls where unupgraded cards fail.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Constellation Bonuses">
    Every 125,000 rolls, you earn stars to spend in the constellation tree. Prioritize the **Luck Path** first to permanently increase your roll rates, followed by damage and roll speed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Milestone | Reward / Unlocks | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **25 Rolls** | Auto-Roll Feature | Keeps rolling automated while managing inventory |
| **100 Rolls** | Quick Roll Feature | Speeds up card generation significantly |
| **125k Rolls** | First Constellation Star | Invest directly into the Luck Path for better pulls |
| **Wave 50** | Increased Resource Yields | Upgrade your main carry to comfortably clear this wall |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Conservation">
Avoid spending Trait Rerolls or high-tier potions during your first 1,000 rolls. These resources are extremely scarce and should only be used on long-term S-tier cards.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Optimal Lineup and Team Building">

A successful lineup in Card Chronicles requires more than just equipping your rarest cards. You have up to five active card slots, allowing you to create highly specialized teams. Balancing raw damage, utility, and luck-boosting passives is the key to conquering endless waves and tough bosses.

  <Card title="Wave Carry Build">

    - **Secondary DPS**: A merged combat card for consistent damage.
    - **Utility Support**: Cards that reduce enemy damage or buff attack speed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Luck Farming Build">
    - **Luck Passives**: Cards with traits like **Divine Blessing** or **Fortune Infusion**.
    - **Goal**: Maximize base luck multipliers up to 36x or higher.
    - **Use Case**: Equip this slot strictly when rolling for summons.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Boss Rush Build">
    - **Single-Target DPS**: High-impact cards with defensive ignore.
    - **Defense Mitigation**: Cards that reduce boss damage by 15% or more.
    - **Survival**: High HP cards to endure long boss fights.
  </Card>
