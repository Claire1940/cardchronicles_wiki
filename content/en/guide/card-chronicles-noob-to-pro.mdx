<Section icon={Target} title="The 5-Step Progression Roadmap">

To transition from a newcomer to a professional player, you must follow a structured path. Spreading your resources too thin is the fastest way to stall your progress. Follow these steps to ensure you are always moving toward the endgame.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Initial Roll Grind">
    Start by using any available "Release" codes to get basic Luck Potions. Set your game to auto-roll and focus on filling your initial lineup slots. At this stage, any card with a higher rarity than "Common" is a massive upgrade. Do not spend any Trait Rerolls yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Your First Carry">
    Once you pull a Legendary or high-tier Epic card with a strong damage-dealing ability, this becomes your "Carry." Spend your initial gold and materials to upgrade this single card. A single level 50 card is often stronger than five level 10 cards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlocking Ability Synergies">
    As you push through higher waves, enemies will become tankier. Check your cards' abilities. Look for cards that provide AoE (Area of Effect) damage or debuffs. A pro lineup always includes at least one card dedicated to clearing trash mobs quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The 25k Roll Milestone">
    This is the turning point. Many powerful codes, like **Tyfor400kvisits!**, require 25,000 rolls to redeem. Once you hit this, you will receive a massive influx of Potions and Trait Rerolls. This is where you begin "fixing" your best cards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Trait Optimization">
    Now that you have S-Tier cards and a stockpile of rerolls, start rolling for offensive traits like **Damage%** or **Crit Rate**. A pro player never settles for a defensive trait on a primary damage dealer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Never use Trait Rerolls on cards you plan to replace within 24 hours. These are the most valuable items in the game—save them for cards that appear at the top of the current meta tier list.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Mechanics: Traits and Chronogems">

Once you have mastered the basics of the **card chronicles noob to pro** journey, you must look at the hidden stats. Chronogems are a premium-style currency often given out in reward bundles. These should be spent on permanent upgrades that affect every card you own, such as global damage increases or permanent roll speed boosts.

Traits, on the other hand, are card-specific. When you use a Trait Reroll, you are looking for specific keywords that multiply your power.

**Top Tier Traits to Watch For:**
- **Godly**: Massive boost to all stats (The ultimate goal).
- **Celestial**: High damage and speed scaling.
- **Demonic**: High risk, extremely high damage output.
- **Swift**: Essential for supports to get their abilities off faster.
