The Constellation system is a permanent skill tree that players unlock as they roll cards. For every 125,000 rolls completed, or by finishing specific in-game achievements, players earn Star Points. If you want to know card chronicles how to get more luck, your first and most important long-term goal should be investing fully into the luck path.

Additionally, the Card Index tracks all unique cards you have rolled. Reaching specific collection milestones grants permanent luck boosts (such as a flat +1.5 luck increase). Always check your index regularly to claim these rewards, as they form the foundation of your base luck multiplier.
