<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Rolling Mechanics and Rarity System">

The heart of the experience lies in the rolling system. Every roll is a chance to find a game-changing unit, but understanding the value of what you pull is more important than the rarity color itself. In Card Chronicles RNG, an upgraded A-tier card will often outperform a fresh S-tier card that lacks investment.

| Rarity | Battle Role | Upgrade Priority | Long-Term Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **S-Tier (Mythic)** | Main Carry / Boss Killer | Highest | Essential for late-game |
| **A-Tier (Legendary)** | Reliable DPS / Support | High | Strong mid-to-late filler |
| **B-Tier (Epic)** | Specialist / Role Filler | Medium | Temporary wave support |
| **C-Tier (Rare)** | Starter Fodder | Low | Replace as soon as possible |

Rarity defines the ceiling of a card's potential, but the "floor" is determined by your upgrades. When you pull a new card, compare its base stats and abilities to your currently upgraded units. If the new card doesn't offer a significant utility boost or a path to higher damage, it might be better used as upgrade fodder for your existing core team.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Never spend your Trait Rerolls on C-tier or B-tier cards. These resources are scarce, and you will regret using them when you finally pull a Mythic unit that needs a specific stat to become viable.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Card Tier List and Lineup Building">

Building the best lineup in Card Chronicles RNG isn't about having five S-tier cards; it's about synergy. You need a balance of area-of-effect (AOE) damage for waves and high single-target damage for the "Strong Foes" that appear periodically.

### 2026 Meta Rankings
- **S-Tier**: High-rarity units with multi-hit abilities. These are your primary investment targets.
- **A-Tier**: Reliable units that offer consistent performance. Great for filling slots while waiting for Mythic pulls.
- **B-Tier**: Situational specialists. Some B-tier cards have unique debuffs that make them useful for specific boss fights.
- **C-Tier**: Immediate replacements. Only use these in the first 10 minutes of gameplay.

| Build Archetype | Core Roles | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wave Clearer** | 3x AOE DPS / 2x Speed | Rapid gem farming |
| **Boss Crusher** | 2x Single Target / 1x Buffer | Pushing new wave records |
| **Balanced** | 2x AOE / 1x Single / 2x Utility | General progression |

<Callout type="tip" title="Lineup Logic">
If you find yourself failing at a specific wave, check if your cards are hitting the right targets. Sometimes swapping a high-rarity AOE unit for a slightly lower-rarity Single Target unit is all you need to kill a boss before the timer expires.
</Callout>
