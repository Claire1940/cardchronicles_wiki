<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Upgrading and Trait Reroll Strategy">

Upgrading your cards is the bridge between a lucky roll and a dominant lineup. Every card in Card Chronicles RNG Anime can be leveled up to unlock stronger versions of its base abilities. However, the real "min-maxing" happens in the Trait system. Traits provide passive buffs like increased crit chance, faster attack speed, or raw damage multipliers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Keeper">
    Before spending resources, ensure the card is at least A-tier or S-tier. Never upgrade a card you plan to replace in the next hour of gameplay.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Level Up First">
    Use your Chronogems to increase the card's level. This provides a flat stat boost that makes the card immediately more effective in wave combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Trait Rerolls">
    Once the card is leveled, use your Trait Rerolls. Aim for traits that complement the card's role (e.g., Attack Speed for AOE sweepers).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Advanced Abilities">
    At specific level milestones, cards unlock secondary abilities. These often change the card's utility entirely, allowing for more complex team builds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Trait Rerolls are a finite resource, especially once you have exhausted the initial code rewards. You should treat them as "endgame currency." The most common mistake players make is burning 50 rerolls on a mid-tier card, only to roll a top-tier card five minutes later with no rerolls left to optimize it.

| Trait Type | Effect | Best For | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Godly** | Massive all-around stat boost | S-Tier Carries | ★★★★★ |
| **Swift** | Significant Attack Speed increase | AOE Units | ★★★★☆ |
| **Brutal** | High Critical Damage multiplier | Boss Killers | ★★★★☆ |
| **Lucky** | Increases drop rates/rarity chance | Farming Units | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Sturdy** | Health and Defense boost | Frontline Units | ★★☆☆☆ |

<Callout type="success" title="The 25k Milestone">

When you hit a "wall" (a wave you cannot clear), stop pushing. Instead, drop back to a lower wave that you can clear quickly and farm it for resources. Use these resources to upgrade your weakest card slot. Progression in this game is a marathon, not a sprint; consistent farming is always more effective than repeatedly failing a difficult boss fight.

<Callout type="info" title="Farming Efficiency">
If it takes you more than 30 seconds to clear a wave, you are farming inefficiently. Drop down 5-10 waves until your clear time is under 15 seconds to maximize your resource-per-hour gain.
