<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding the RNG: Rolling Mechanics and Luck Systems">

The core loop of Card Chronicles revolves around rolling for anime-style cards, upgrading their abilities, and deploying them to fight endless waves of enemies. Progression is heavily tied to how effectively you manage your rolling resources and luck multipliers. Unlike basic luck-based games, this Roblox experience rewards players who understand how to stack multipliers and time their rolling sessions.

Your base luck determines the initial probability of pulling rare cards, but this stat can be significantly boosted using potions and specific reward codes. To make the most of your resources, you must understand the distinction between different boost items and when to activate them.

| Potion Type | Category | Core Effect | Strategic Timing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Luck Potion** | Rolling Boost | Increases the chance of pulling higher-rarity cards | Use during active, long rolling sessions |
| **Super Luck Potion** | High-Value Boost | Provides a massive multiplier to rare card acquisition | Pair with Roll Speed Potions for maximum output |
| **Roll Speed Potion** | Rolling Speed | Decreases the time required between consecutive rolls | Best used when clearing inventory space |
| **Border Chance Potion** | Card Bonus Chase | Enhances the probability of pulling cards with special borders | Save for late-game min-maxing sessions |
| **Battle Speed Potion** | Combat Pace | Speeds up the wave clearing process in active combat | Use when farming materials to fund more rolls |
| **Boss Potion** | Progression Boost | Increases combat capabilities against strong foes | Activate when pushing past difficult wave walls |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Idle Waste">
Never activate high-value boosters like Super Luck Potions or Roll Speed Potions if you plan to walk away from your device. These items run on real-time countdowns, meaning any idle time directly translates to wasted progression value.
</Callout>

Rolling volume is the single most important factor in long-term success. The more rolls you execute, the higher your chances of hitting the pity thresholds or securing a high-tier carry card. Therefore, increasing your rolling speed is just as important as increasing your luck multiplier.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Managing Trait Rerolls and Keeper Cards">

Traits are unique modifiers applied to your cards that can completely alter their performance in combat. While rolling determines the base card and its rarity, traits determine the final efficiency of that card in high-level wave clearing and boss fights. Trait Rerolls are highly sought after, making their proper management a critical aspect of your progression strategy.

Because Trait Rerolls are limited, they should never be spent on temporary or low-rarity cards. The game provides generous amounts of rerolls through codes like `AvailableForEveryoneNow` (which grants 100 Trait Rerolls), but these resources will vanish quickly if distributed poorly across your collection.

<Section icon={Target} title="Upgrades, Abilities, and Lineup Optimization">

A common pitfall for players is focusing entirely on luck and card rarity while neglecting the upgrade system. A lower-rarity card that has been heavily upgraded and possesses unlocked abilities will consistently outperform a fresh, unupgraded rare pull. Upgrades serve as the bridge between raw rolling luck and actual combat progress.

When you roll duplicate cards, they are not wasted. Use these duplicates to strengthen your primary combat units, unlocking new abilities that enhance their combat value. Focus your upgrades on a single carry card first, rather than spreading your gold and materials evenly across your entire lineup.

| Build Name | Core Focus | Strategy | Upgrade Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Wave Clear** | High-area damage | Clear weak enemies quickly to farm resources | Main carry card gets 80% of resources |
| **Boss Push Build** | Single-target DPS | Defeat strong foes at critical wave milestones | Focus on high-damage single-target units |
| **Balanced Progress** | Damage and utility | Maintain steady progression through endless mode | Even split between carry and support units |

<Callout type="tip" title="Upgrade Before Rerolling">
Always invest in upgrading your card's base level and unlocking its core abilities before you begin fine-tuning its traits. A card's base stats scale with upgrades, making the percent-based multipliers from traits far more effective on high-level units.
</Callout>

Once your primary carry card is fully upgraded, you can begin introducing support units that provide crowd control or damage buffs, creating a balanced team capable of surviving the scaling difficulty of endless waves.

</Section>
