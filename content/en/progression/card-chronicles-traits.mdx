<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Optimizing Traits">

Optimizing your **card chronicles traits** requires a systematic approach. If you reroll aimlessly, you will find yourself out of resources with a lineup of mediocre cards. Follow this professional workflow to ensure every reroll moves your account forward.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Your Carry">
    Before spending a single reroll, identify which card in your current lineup is doing the most work. This is usually your most upgraded card or the one with the highest base rarity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Roll Requirements">
    Ensure you have met any necessary milestones. Some high-value rewards and traits might be more effective once you have cleared the 25,000 roll gate, unlocking better potion synergies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Luck Potions">
    While Luck Potions primarily affect card rolling, using them in conjunction with a focused session of card acquisition ensures you have the best "base" to apply your traits to.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Rerolls in Batches">
    Don't do one roll at a time. Use your Trait Rerolls in batches of 10-20. This allows you to see the variance in modifiers and stop when you hit a "good enough" trait for your current progression level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Lock and Upgrade">
    Once you land a high-tier trait, focus all your remaining gold and upgrade materials into that card. A card with an S-tier trait and max upgrades is your ticket to the end-game.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Modifier Stacking">
Remember that traits stack with card abilities. If a card already has a 10% speed boost ability, a speed trait will make it an absolute monster on the battlefield.
</Callout>
