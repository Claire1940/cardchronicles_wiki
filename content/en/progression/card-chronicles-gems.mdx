<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Farming Strategy: Maximizing Gem Efficiency">

Effective farming in Card Chronicles requires a balance between active play and smart resource allocation. You cannot simply roll endlessly and expect to reach the leaderboards; you must utilize your **card chronicles gems** to buy potions that multiply your efforts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Roll Volume">
    Start by using **Roll Speed Potions** to increase your total roll count. This is essential for unlocking the 25,000-roll gate required for the best codes. Do not worry about rarity yet; just focus on volume.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Lineup Stabilization">
    Once you have a few decent cards, build a lineup and start pushing **Endless Waves**. This provides a steady stream of basic resources and helps you identify which cards are worth your gems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Concentrated Rolling">
    Save your **Super Luck Potions** and **Luck Potions** for a single, focused session. Activate them simultaneously to maximize the chance of pulling a Mythic or Secret card.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trait Optimization">
    After pulling a "Keeper Card," spend your **Trait Rerolls**. This is where your gems provide the most value, as a good trait can double a card's combat effectiveness.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Avoid spending gems on **Battle Speed Potions** in the very early game. These are better used when you have a strong lineup and want to farm high-level waves quickly. Early on, roll volume is more important than combat speed.
</Callout>

</Section>
