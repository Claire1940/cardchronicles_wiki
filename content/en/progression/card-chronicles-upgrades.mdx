<Section icon={Target} title="How to Upgrade Cards: Step-by-Step Progression">

Navigating your progression route requires a structured approach to avoid wasting valuable resources like Trait Rerolls and Chronogems. Follow this step-by-step walkthrough to systematically build an unstoppable team.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Roll for Usable Cards">
    Begin by rolling to build a decent starting card pool. Do not immediately focus on perfect rarities; look for cards that can form a working lineup and help you clear the first few wave thresholds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Form a Small Fighting Lineup">
    Place your strongest available cards into your active combat slots. Prioritize cards that offer immediate battle value over flashy collection-only cards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade Focused Carry Cards">
    Select your primary damage dealer and invest your resources to level them up. A single highly upgraded carry card will push waves much faster than a team of weak, unupgraded cards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Combat Abilities">
    Progress through the Tower of Beginnings to unlock card abilities. These skills, such as stuns or multi-target strikes, are vital for overcoming tough wave walls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Apply Trait Rerolls to Keeper Cards">
    Once you have a card that will remain in your lineup for a long time, use your Trait Rerolls to find powerful traits like Chronoger or Astral Overlord.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Reroll Waste">

When you roll a rare card, compare its base stats and potential abilities against your current upgraded cards. Spreading upgrades evenly across your entire collection is a recipe for progression stagnation.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Maximizing Upgrade Efficiency with Potions and Boosts">

To speed up your card chronicles upgrades, you must effectively utilize the game's potion and boost systems. Redeeming active codes and completing daily milestones will reward you with a variety of potions that increase your luck, roll speed, and battle speed. Using these items during planned, focused gameplay sessions is the most efficient way to gather upgrade materials and rare duplicates.

Luck Potions and Super Luck Potions increase your chances of rolling high-rarity cards, which can either replace your current lineup or be recycled to upgrade your existing cards. Roll Speed Potions allow you to process more rolls in less time, maximizing the duration of your active luck boosts. Meanwhile, Battle Speed Potions and Boss Potions help you farm waves and defeat strong foes much faster, yielding more gold and resources for your upgrades.
