<Section icon={Target} title="Section 3: Beginner Progression Steps">

Starting out in a card RNG game can be overwhelming, especially when resources are limited. To ensure you do not waste your early-game boosts and potions, follow a structured progression path. Your first priority should be expanding your card pool, followed by upgrading a reliable wave-clearing team, and finally optimizing traits once you have secured permanent carries.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summon Your First Lineup">
    Begin by rolling for basic anime cards to fill your active combat slots. Focus on establishing a full team rather than hunting for ultra-rare cards immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Tower of Beginnings">
    Take your initial lineup into the Tower of Beginnings. Defeating floor 6 is critical as it unlocks the highly efficient **Infinite Mode** and dual-slot battles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade Your Carry Cards">
    Invest your gold and resources into upgrading cards that have strong multi-target or stun abilities. Do not spread your upgrades evenly; focus on one or two main carries.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Boosts Strategically">
    Only use your Luck and Roll Speed Potions when you are ready for a focused, active rolling session. Never activate boosts right before going idle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Apply Trait Rerolls to Keepers">
    Once you obtain a high-rarity card (S-tier) that will remain in your lineup for a long time, use your accumulated **Trait Rerolls** to secure powerful combat buffs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

By following these steps, you avoid turning your inventory into a collection of half-upgraded cards that cannot clear high-level waves. Progression is all about focusing your resources on cards that actually win fights.
