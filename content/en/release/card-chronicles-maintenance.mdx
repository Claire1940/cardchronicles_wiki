<Section icon={Target} title="How to Prepare for Scheduled Downtime">

To minimize the impact of **card chronicles maintenance** on your progression, you should follow a specific routine before the servers go dark. Since the game revolves around rolling and resource management, being caught in the middle of a high-value session can be frustrating.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Cease Potion Usage">
    Stop activating Luck Potions, Roll Speed Potions, or Super Luck Potions if the maintenance countdown is less than the duration of the boost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Finalize Current Waves">
    Finish your current wave push. Rewards for wave progression are typically saved upon completion of a stage, so don't start a long boss fight right before a shutdown.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Review Your Lineup">
    Ensure your strongest cards are equipped in your main lineup. While data is backed up, having your primary team active is a good safety measure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check Trait Rerolls">
    If you have pending Trait Rerolls, use them or save them. Avoid clicking "Reroll" in the final seconds to prevent a "ghost roll" where resources are spent but the trait doesn't update.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
If you are mid-roll when the server shuts down, the game typically saves your last successful roll. However, it is always safer to stop rolling manually when the timer hits the 1-minute mark.
</Callout>

</Section>
