<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression & Rolling Strategy">

Succeeding in the latest **card chronicles update** requires a structured approach to progression. Spreading your resources too thin across multiple low-tier cards is a common pitfall that can stall your progress against tougher wave milestones. Instead, follow this optimal progression path to maximize your battle efficiency and upgrade speed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Roll for Usable Base Cards">
    Begin by rolling to build your initial card pool. Do not worry about finding ultra-rare cards immediately; focus on securing a few reliable combat units to form a working lineup.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Form a Balanced Fighting Lineup">
    Place your strongest pulled cards into your active lineup. Prioritize cards that offer multi-target damage or useful combat abilities over cards that only look visually rare in your collection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade Winning Cards First">
    Invest your gold and merge duplicates into the cards that consistently clear waves and defeat strong foes. Upgrading unlocks critical combat abilities that scale your damage output.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Boosts in Focused Sessions">
    Only activate your Luck, Roll Speed, and Super Luck Potions when you are prepared to actively roll for the entire duration of the boost. Wasting active potion time while idle slows progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Apply Trait Rerolls Strategically">
    Once you identify a 'keeper' card that will remain in your lineup long-term, use your Trait Rerolls to secure powerful combat passives. Avoid wasting rerolls on temporary placeholder cards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

By adhering to this loop, you ensure that every resource spent directly contributes to overcoming the next wave wall. Keep track of your progression milestones using the structured table below.

| Progression Phase | Core Objective | Key Activities | Resource Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Game** | Build initial lineup | Roll for base cards, push early waves | Save Trait Rerolls, use basic potions |
| **Mid Game** | Unlock card abilities | Merge duplicate cards, push wave milestones | Spend potions in focused sessions |
| **Late Game** | Maximize team synergy | Reroll traits on S-tier keeper cards | Optimize traits, spend Chronogems |

<Callout type="tip" title="Constellation Priority">
Every time you experience a new roll cutscene, you earn points in the Constellation system. Prioritize spending these points on Roll Speed and Luck increases first, as they accelerate how quickly you can acquire high-tier cards.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Traits, Rerolls, and Boost Potions Guide">

Traits add an incredibly powerful layer of customization to your cards in Card Chronicles. Unlike simple stat boosts, high-tier traits can introduce entirely new combat mechanics and active abilities to your units. Securing top-tier traits like **Astral Overlord**, **Chrono**, or **Sovereign** can turn a standard carry card into an absolute powerhouse.

However, Trait Rerolls are a finite resource. While the latest codes provide a generous starting pool, you must manage them carefully.

| Potion / Item Name | Category | Primary Effect | Best Activation Timing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Luck Potion** | Rolling Boost | Increases chance of pulling rarer cards | Use during active, long rolling sessions |
| **Super Luck Potion** | High-Value Boost | Provides a massive luck multiplier | Pair with Roll Speed Potion III |
| **Roll Speed Potion** | Rolling Speed | Decreases interval between rolls | Use when clearing large card backlogs |
| **Battle Speed Potion** | Combat Pace | Speeds up combat animations by 15% | Activate when farming waves or bosses |
| **Boss Potion** | Boss Spawn | Increases chance of encountering bosses | Use when team lineup is highly upgraded |
| **Trait Rerolls** | Card Building | Randomly alters a card's passive trait | Spend exclusively on S-tier keeper cards |

To maximize your rolling efficiency, always stack your potions. Activating a **Super Luck Potion** alongside a **Roll Speed Potion** ensures you get the maximum number of high-probability rolls within your active boost window.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Reroll Waste">
Never spend your Trait Rerolls on early placeholder cards. It is incredibly easy to burn through hundreds of rerolls trying to perfect a card that you will ultimately replace a few hours later once your rolling luck improves.
</Callout>
